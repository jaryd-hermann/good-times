# New Prompts for Friends and Family Categories

## Friends Category

### Fun & Lighthearted Questions

1. What's the weirdest thing you've Googled recently?
2. What's a food combination you love that other people think is gross?
3. If you had to wear one outfit for the rest of your life, what would it be?
4. What's something you're weirdly good at that serves no real purpose?
5. What's a conspiracy theory you don't actually believe but find entertaining?
6. What's the most random skill you've picked up from YouTube?
7. If you could only eat one cuisine for the rest of your life, what would it be?
8. What's a movie or show you've seen way too many times?
9. What's something you do when no one's watching that would surprise us?
10. If you had to describe your personality using only emojis, which ones would you pick?
11. What's a superpower that would be completely useless but fun to have?
12. What's the most ridiculous thing you've convinced someone was true?
13. If you could swap lives with any fictional character for a day, who would it be?
14. What's a song you're embarrassed to admit you love?
15. What's something you're way too old to still be doing but do anyway?
16. If you could add one completely useless feature to your phone, what would it be?
17. What's a word you always mispronounce even though you know it's wrong?
18. What's the most unnecessary thing you've bought online recently?
19. If you could make one minor inconvenience illegal, what would it be?
20. What's a hill you're willing to die on that doesn't actually matter?

### Observational & Story-Based Questions

21. What's the funniest thing you've overheard in public recently?
22. What's a moment from this group that lives rent-free in your head?
23. What's something {member_name} does that's uniquely them?
24. If we made a reality show about this group, what would it be called?
25. What's a running joke in this group that never gets old?
26. What's something you've noticed about {member_name} that they probably don't know you noticed?
27. What's the most chaotic thing we've done together as a group?
28. If you had to cast this group in a heist movie, who would play what role?
29. What's a moment where someone in this group surprised you?
30. What's something we always do when we're together that we should document?

### Recommendations & Sharing Questions

31. What's a podcast you're obsessed with right now that we should all listen to?
32. What's a restaurant or food spot you discovered recently that's amazing?
33. What's a YouTube channel or TikTok creator you're low-key addicted to?
34. What's a product under $50 that changed your life?
35. What's a book you read that you can't stop thinking about?
36. What's a TV show you're watching that we should all start?
37. What's a life hack you learned recently that actually works?
38. What's an app you use daily that we probably don't know about?
39. What's a recipe you tried recently that was surprisingly good?
40. What's something you bought that was way better than expected?

### Hypothetical & Creative Questions

41. If you could create a new holiday, what would it celebrate and how would we celebrate it?
42. If you had to teach a class on something you know nothing about, what would it be?
43. What's a business idea you have that's probably terrible but you love anyway?
44. If you could add one rule to society that everyone had to follow, what would it be?
45. What's a completely made-up fact you wish was true?
46. If you could rename any common object, what would you call it?
47. What's a trend you're glad died out?
48. If you could make one thing free for everyone forever, what would it be?
49. What's a skill you'd want to learn if you could instantly master it?
50. If you had to survive a zombie apocalypse with only things from your current location, how screwed are you?

### Personal Quirks & Preferences Questions

51. What's a hill you're willing to die on about something completely trivial?
52. What's something you're way too passionate about for how little it matters?
53. What's a pet peeve you have that's completely irrational?

55. What's a guilty pleasure you're not actually guilty about?
56. What's something you're weirdly competitive about?
